Sir Francis Drake
Heroic English Admiral or Pirate
Drake by his globe
The English generally consider Drake to be a great man who rose from being the son of a middleclass merchant to becoming admiral of the British Navy. He is famous for fighting the Spannish Armada and for sailing around the world. He lived near Plymouth in his house in Buckland Abbey there is a famous drum. If it is beaten it his said that Drake will return from the dead and save Britain one last time.

In Spain he has a very different reputation. He is infamous and known as El Draco and for being a pirate captain who was forever robbing their ships and taking prizes.
